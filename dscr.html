DSCR Rental Loans

Long-term financing based on property cash flow, not personal income. Qualify on the asset, not your tax returns.

Overview

Finance Based on Cash Flow

DSCR (Debt Service Coverage Ratio) loans let you qualify based on your property's rental income relative to the mortgage payment. If the property cash flows, you can get financed. No personal income verification, no tax return headaches.

This is the go-to product for investors building or holding rental portfolios who want stable, long-term debt without the friction of traditional underwriting.

Loan Range

$500,000 to $3,000,000

Term

30-year fixed and adjustable rate options

Qualification

Property cash flow based, no personal income docs

Property Types

SFR, 2-4 unit, condos, townhomes, small multifamily
